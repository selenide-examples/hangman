How to play

Goal: guess the hidden word one letter at a time before the hangman runs out of luck.

  1. Pick a mode: Timed gives you a countdown based on the word length (≈10 s per letter + 5 s). Untimed lets you think as long as you like.
  2. Click a letter (or type it on your keyboard). Correct letters fill the blanks and turn green; wrong letters turn red and the gallows grows.
  3. You get 6 wrong guesses for short words and more for longer words (1 extra per letter beyond 5). Watch the hangman's face: he starts out happy and gets increasingly anxious as things get dicey.
  4. Stuck? Halfway through your allowed wrong guesses the Hint button unlocks and reveals the topic of the word.
  5. Guess every letter (or beat the clock) to win. Run out of guesses or time and it's game over.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • A-Z / А-Я: guess that letter
  • T / U: pick Timed / Untimed mode
  • Alt+H (Windows/Linux) or Option+H (Mac): reveal hint (when unlocked)
  • Enter or Space: play again
  • ?: open / close this guide, Esc: close
